Welcome to the Burro Bar

A neighborhood Mexican kitchen and tequila bar, The Burro Bar is the smaller siblin g of The Painted Burro in Somerville. Our tequila list features over 100 bottles that ar e “Burro tested and approved”. With splashes of influence from Baja and Oaxaca to Veracruz and Yucatan, Chef Joe Cassinelli’s dishes showcase a creative, fun side of familiar flavors, where ingredients and local produce take center stage.
